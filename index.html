GUIDE TO STARTING UP

Date 1 : 10 May 2021

  • Entrepeneurship is not for everyone, so be sure about it.

    HOW TO GENERATE AN IDEA:
  • Look for startup ideas funded in other countries and make them better, enhance it. Then launch it in india.
  • Look for business ideas that has very low Net Promoter Score, and make it better
  • Look for business ideas that were very popular in the past, but got shutdown overtime. Enhance them. (Eg radio was very popular, nowadays spotify is very popular).

    HOW TO INITIATE WORKING ON A STARTUP (PRODUCT IS NOT YET READY)
  • Choose a name for the startup (It gives a sense of dedication and attachment)
  • Create a professional logo
  • Think of a one-line description of what your product does, and one line description of what problem it solves.
  • Buy a domain name and build a website
  • Get email address of customers that are willing to try out a demo of the product.

    HOW TO FILTER THE BEST IDEA OF DOZEN OF IDEAS IN YOUR HEAD
  • Run campaigns/digital ads on social media sites
  • Look the CPR, CPC, CPA of all 2-3 three ideas.
  • Look for the one with high CPR, and less CPC and CPA.
